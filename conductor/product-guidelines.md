# Product Guidelines - React Native Template

## Prose Style & Tone
- **Professional & Concise:** Communication should be direct, efficient, and technically accurate. Avoid unnecessary filler or overly conversational language.
- **Clarity Above All:** Prioritize clear instructions and precise terminology in both documentation and code comments.

## Visual Branding
- **Minimalist & High-Contrast:** Focus on a clean aesthetic with ample white space, high-contrast typography, and a single, strong accent color to guide the user's eye.
- **Content-Focused:** The design should stay out of the way, letting the application's content and functionality take center stage.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Speed & Responsiveness:** The application must feel fast. Optimize for quick transitions, immediate feedback on user actions, and smooth animations.
- **Consistency & Familiarity:** Adhere to established mobile UI patterns (Human Interface Guidelines for iOS, Material Design for Android) to reduce the user's learning curve.
- **Efficiency & Reduced Friction:** Streamline user flows to minimize the steps required to complete common tasks.

## Accessibility (A11y)
- **Accessibility-First:** Accessibility is not an afterthought. Every component and feature should be designed with accessibility in mind from the beginning.
- **Inclusive Design:** Ensure full support for screen readers (VoiceOver/TalkBack), proper color contrast ratios, and dynamic text scaling.
